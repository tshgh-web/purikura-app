✦ PRIVACY POLICY
✦ Last updated: June 2025
1. Overview

Purikura Booth ("the App") is a browser-based photo booth experience. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is — and importantly, what is not — collected when you use this App.

2. No Photos Are Collected or Stored

All photos you take using this App are processed entirely within your browser using the Web Camera API and HTML5 Canvas. Your photos are held temporarily in your browser's memory only for the duration of your session and are permanently deleted when you close or refresh the tab.

We do not transmit, upload, store, or have access to any photos, images, or video captured through this App. No photos are ever sent to any server.

3. Camera Access

The App requests access to your device's camera solely to provide the photo booth functionality. Your browser will prompt you to grant or deny this permission. Camera access is used in real-time within your browser only; the video stream is never recorded, saved, or transmitted externally.

You may revoke camera access at any time through your browser's settings.

4. No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect, process, or store any personal information, including your name, email address, location, device identifiers, or usage data. The App does not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics scripts, advertising networks, or any third-party data collection services.

5. Third-Party Services

The App loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) for visual styling. Google's own privacy policy governs any data associated with font delivery. No other third-party services are used.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for general audiences. Because we collect no personal data whatsoever, the App does not pose specific privacy risks to children. However, parents and guardians are encouraged to supervise use and ensure appropriate content is captured.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected here with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
